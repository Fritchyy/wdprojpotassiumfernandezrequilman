Kitty Adventure!

About us

Our website is designed for cat enthusiasts like us to enjoy a nice and sweet time with cute and cuddly cats. This website’s premise is simply about taking care of cats and making sure they grow healthy akin to taking care of an actual real life cat. There are different cat breeds to choose from. Different cat breeds may have differing personalities as well that would make them respond differently to your petting so you better collect them all and have fun spending time with these silly furballs!

Guide

Take care of the default cats we’ve provided by clicking the feed, water, or bathe button when the cat's hunger, thirst, or hygiene meter gets low. By doing so, players can earn coins. You can also earn couns by playing minigames and teach your cat tricks using the Train button. These coins allow players to buy boxes to have a chance at getting new cat breeds using the Other cats! button or liven up the screen by purchasing wallpapers or outfits using the Shop button. You can equip these purchased cats, wallpapers, or outfit via the Wardrobe button. You can even give your pet love and affection using the Pet button in which you'll see its reaction to your petitng! Finally, you can view your milestones via the Achievement button.

If the font is not to your liking, click the settings button where you can adjust its type, size, and color!
